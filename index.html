About Us

A GUY I KNOW - CEBU, INC. was established in 2015. Back then, we were a group of freelancers working together to deliver graphics, animations, and eLearning outputs to Australia-based clients. With our inevitable success, we have grown and became an established company. We are skilled in hands-on development of interactive content using a multitude of industry standard tools. These include EnableNow (which includes WebAssist), Articulate 360, Camtasia, Adobe Captivate, Ancile Prodpak, Multiple LMS (Learning Management systems), and the full Adobe Design Suite.

WHITEBELT INC. is an all in one platform used by hundreds of martial arts schools in Australia. From Attendance, Grading, Reports and Membership, WHITEBELT offers it for free to all martial arts schools.
